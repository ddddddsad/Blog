Research Experience

May 2023 - Present: Galaxy Image Analysis Research with China Space Station Telescope (CSST) (Principal Investigator). My responsibilities include:

  • Searching for large samples of multi-band photometric images of various types of galaxies (merging galaxies, elliptical galaxies, spiral/barred spiral galaxies, Edge-On galaxies) and constructing a multi-type galaxy photometric image dataset suitable for machine learning, including training, testing, and validation sets.
  • Developing a model to distinguish merging galaxies using a hybrid machine learning method (neural network feature extraction and support vector machine classification), and optimizing the algorithm and model architecture to achieve high performance.
  • Compiling the research into a paper for submission to a journal.

May 2022 - May 2023: Observational Study of Galaxy Evolution Along Merger Sequence: Gas, Star Formation, and Nuclear Activity (Core Member). My work involved searching for multi-band photometric data (SDSS-2MASS-Herschel) of Herschel KPAIRS samples, using CIGALE and MAGPHYS for SED fitting and stellar population decomposition, and statistically analyzing parameters such as gas content, galaxy mass, and age at different stages of evolution and in different components of merging galaxies.

October 2021 - May 2022: Remote Sensing Mineralogy Study of Young Lunar Basalts (Member). I participated in searching for coordinates, diameters, and frequency data of impact craters in the South Pole-Aitken Basin on the Moon and identifying visible-near-infrared spectra of materials in this region.
